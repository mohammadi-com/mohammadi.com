I wanted this website to feel closer to the weblogs I actually read: direct,
quiet, and centered on writing.

The structure is simple. The home page is the blog index, the about page holds
the durable personal context, and everything else should earn its place over
time.

The implementation follows [AstroPaper](https://github.com/satnaing/astro-paper)
and borrows the blog-first shape I liked from
[Peter Steinberger's website](https://github.com/steipete/steipete.me).

That is the whole point for now: a small public home base that can grow through
posts instead of becoming another hand-maintained profile page.
