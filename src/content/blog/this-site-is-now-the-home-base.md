Social profiles are useful, but they are not a home. This site is the place where my projects, notes, and durable links can live together.

The first version is intentionally simple: a fast static site, a small set of durable pages, and enough structure to grow into a proper writing archive.

The current shape reflects what I keep coming back to in public work: backend systems, Python and FastAPI, automation, TypeScript interfaces, and tools that help people move through the job-search process with less friction.

Next, I want this to become a more useful record of what I am building: deeper project pages, sharper notes, and links to the work that deserves a longer shelf life.
